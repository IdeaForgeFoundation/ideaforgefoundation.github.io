Signature Projects & Initiatives

Beyond our core offerings, we pursue unique, community-focused projects that leverage our members' skills for the greater good.

E-Waste Upcycling Program

We plan to establish a collection point for electronic waste. Our members will learn to safely disassemble, sort, and upcycle components for art projects, repairs, and new inventions, while responsibly recycling the rest. This reduces landfill waste and creates a valuable resource stream.

Status: Seeking corporate partners for equipment donation.

Community Mesh Network

Leveraging amateur radio licenses (HAM) and modern hardware, we aim to build a resilient, open-source telecommunications network. This project provides invaluable hands-on learning in networking and infrastructure, with potential applications in emergency communications and bridging the digital divide.

Status: Initial research and tower site analysis underway.

Our Guiding Principles

Idea Forge Foundation operates with a clear and unwavering set of values. These principles are our moral compass, ensuring we always serve our community's best interests.

Community-Centric

We prioritize the needs and interests of our community above all, empowering members to take ownership.

Board Stewardship

The board acts as stewards of the mission, upholding integrity and transparency without personal gain.

Operational Autonomy

The board refrains from micromanaging daily operations, trusting the community's expertise.

Safety & Well-being

We maintain a supportive and inclusive environment where all can learn and experiment without fear.

Financial Prudence

We treat money like "toxic waste"‚Äîhandled carefully, responsibly, and only when necessary for the community's benefit.

Reciprocity & Giving Back

We believe in a mutual exchange of privileges and giving back to the community that supports us.
