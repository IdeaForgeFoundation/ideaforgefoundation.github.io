When you join Idea Forge, you can opt to become a Community Builder by adding a small Pay It Forward contribution to your membership. 100% of these funds go into a bucket used to offset fees and provide discounted, affiliated makerspace access for those who need it.

Open Innovation Lab (OIL)

OIL is a collaborative, standing program dedicated to open-source hardware and community-driven tech projects. We operate on a model of active contribution.

Active Contributors: Members actively building, breaking, or mentoring within a current lab project maintain full access to lab resources and voting rights on project directions.

Alumni Network: We know that life, jobs, and side projects ebb and flow. If you step away from active lab projects for a season, your status gracefully transitions to Alumni. You are always welcome in our community channels and can reactivate your lab access whenever you are ready to dive back into a build.

Our Mission

To provide a safe and creative environment for individuals of all ages to develop, explore, and share knowledge through the use of technology, tools, and materials in a collaborative, welcoming atmosphere that encourages innovation, learning, and the pursuit of creative expression.

Our Guiding Principles

As members of the Electronic Frontier Alliance, we are committed to upholding core principles that ensure technology supports freedom, justice, and innovation for all. These values are the bedrock of our mission.

Free Expression

People should be able to speak their minds to whoever will listen.

Security

Technology should be trustworthy and answer to its users.

Privacy

Technology should allow private and anonymous speech, and allow users to set their own parameters about what to share with whom.

Creativity

Technology should promote progress by allowing people to build on the ideas, creations, and inventions of others.

Access to Knowledge

Curiosity should be rewarded, not stifled. We uphold these principles by fighting for transparency and freedom in culture, code, and law.
