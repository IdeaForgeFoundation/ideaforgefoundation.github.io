Our Mission: The Spark of Innovation

The Idea Forge Foundation was founded on the belief that a single idea can ignite change. Our mission is to build a vibrant ecosystem for innovation across Oklahoma. We strive to break down barriers to entry for aspiring entrepreneurs and provide a structured pathway from concept to market, ensuring that the best ideas have the support they need to flourish and strengthen our communities.

Our Guiding Principles

As members of the Electronic Frontier Alliance, we are committed to upholding core principles that ensure technology supports freedom, justice, and innovation for all. These values are the bedrock of our mission.

Free Expression

People should be able to speak their minds to whoever will listen.

Security

Technology should be trustworthy and answer to its users.

Privacy

Technology should allow private and anonymous speech, and allow users to set their own parameters about what to share with whom.

Creativity

Technology should promote progress by allowing people to build on the ideas, creations, and inventions of others.

Access to Knowledge

Curiosity should be rewarded, not stifled. We uphold these principles by fighting for transparency and freedom in culture, code, and law.

Our Programs

We offer a suite of programs designed to support innovators at every stage of their journey.

The Spark Incubator

Have a groundbreaking idea? Our incubator provides the initial seed funding, mentorship, and validation resources to transform your concept into a viable project.

The Anvil Accelerator

For early-stage startups ready to scale. This intensive program offers hands-on guidance, access to our network, and strategic support to accelerate your growth.

Community Workshops

Open to all, our workshops cover essential topics from business planning and digital marketing to intellectual property, led by industry experts.
