The Maker Passport

The local tech and maker community is packed with incredible, specialized spaces, but it can be hard to know where to start. The Maker Passport is a physical, collectible guide designed to help you explore the ecosystem.

Grab a passport and visit our affiliated creative spaces, hackerspaces, and labs. Take a tour, connect with their community, and collect a unique stamp at each location. It's a low-friction, highly engaging way to cross-pollinate ideas and discover the specific tools, tech meetups, and talent you need for your next project.
