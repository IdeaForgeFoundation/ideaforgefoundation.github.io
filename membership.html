Become a Member

Join the Idea Forge Foundation community. Membership provides access to our resources, including our upcoming makerspace, and supports our mission to empower Oklahoma's innovators.

Membership Benefits

  • Makerspace Access: Get hands-on with tools and equipment in our partner woodshop and future facilities.
  • Discounted Rates: Special pricing available for needs-based members and initiative participants.
  • Community Events: Priority access to workshops, hackathons like Oklathon, and networking events.
  • Support Innovation: Your membership dues directly fund programs that build Oklahoma's creative future.
