# Idea Forge Foundation: Affiliate Discount & Partnership Policies

## 1. Affiliate Discount Eligibility Policy
The Idea Forge Foundation partners with local fabrication facilities and makerspaces to provide members with access to specialized equipment. To ensure these discounts advance our charitable mission of education and active community innovation, the 25% Affiliate Makerspace Discount is not a guaranteed retail perk of standard membership. It is an earned benefit tied to active participation.

**Eligibility Pathways:**
Members may unlock the 25% Affiliate Discount via one of the following pathways:

* **The Maker Passport Unlock:** Members who actively explore the local tech ecosystem and collect a minimum of three (3) verified stamps from different affiliated Ecosystem Outposts (e.g., Project 3810, Starspace46, The Verge OKC) in their official Maker Passport will be granted the discount code.
* **Open Innovation Lab (OIL) Active Contribution:** Members actively maintaining "Active Contributor" status—defined as physically building, coding, or mentoring on a recognized OIL project—receive the discount to subsidize their fabrication costs for the duration of the project. If a member transitions to "Alumni" status, the physical facility discount is paused.
* **Community Leadership Bounty:** Members who formally volunteer to host a workshop, lead a specialized user group, or provide structured mentorship within the Spark Incubator or Anvil Accelerator are granted a rolling 6-month discount in exchange for their service to the Foundation.
* **Foundation Engagement & Meeting Participation:** The Foundation runs on the energy of those who show up. Members who consistently attend our weekly/monthly meetings and actively contribute to initiatives outside the lab can earn "Active Participant" status. This status is renewed via a brief quarterly opt-in to verify ongoing attendance and community contribution, unlocking the discount for the subsequent quarter.

## 2. Quid Pro Quo Tax Disclosure (For Automated Receipts)
*Note: This text must be appended to the bottom of all automated email receipts sent to members upon payment of their Idea Forge membership dues.*

> **Tax Deductibility Statement:**
> The Idea Forge Foundation is a registered 501(c)(3) non-profit organization. Your membership dues support our open-source programs and community labs.
>
> *Please Note:* If you utilized an Idea Forge Foundation Affiliate Discount to reduce your membership fees at a partner facility, the IRS requires you to deduct the Fair Market Value (FMV) of that discount from your charitable contribution. (For example, if you saved $100 this year using our discount at a partner makerspace, the first $100 of your Idea Forge dues are considered a transaction for goods/services and are not tax-deductible). Please consult your tax professional regarding your specific deductions.

## 3. Conflict of Interest Addendum: Ecosystem Partnerships
*Note: This addendum must be signed annually by all board members and key personnel.*

**Policy Statement:**
The Idea Forge Foundation frequently establishes formal partnerships, Memorandums of Understanding (MOUs), and discount pipelines with external organizations, including for-profit makerspaces, tech hubs, and accelerators. To protect the Foundation’s tax-exempt status, no board member, officer, or key employee may personally benefit financially from the routing of Foundation members or funds to a partner organization.

**Enforcement:**
1.  **Mandatory Disclosure:** Any board member holding an ownership stake, equity, employment contract, or direct financial interest in a prospective or current partner organization must fully disclose this relationship in writing to the Board.
2.  **Recusal:** The conflicted board member must recuse themselves from all discussions, negotiations, and formal votes regarding partnerships, MOUs, or financial arrangements involving that specific organization.
3.  **Incidental Benefit:** All partnerships must be structured so that any financial benefit to the external partner organization is strictly incidental to the broader educational and charitable mission of the Idea Forge Foundation.
